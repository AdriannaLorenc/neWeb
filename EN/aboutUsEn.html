ABOUT THE COMPANY

My construction company has been successfully operating in the Swedish market for over a decade.
I started my business in 2000 as a Polish sole proprietor, supplying general construction services. On 19 th May 2005, my firm was registered in Sweden under the name of Jano Bygg c/o Janusz Gawel, and in January 2014 it was transformed into a joint-stock company as Jano Bygg AB .

At present, the company employs several qualified workers with many years’ experience on the Swedish market. We strive to maintain good relations with our customers and we do our best to provide everyone with the confidence that the services are carried out in a professional manner.

Jano Bygg AB’s customers are primarily individual developers but I also fulfil contracts for large companies as the main contractor or a subcontractor. Our customers are not only satisfied with the quality of the work carried out but also with the quality of the materials used as well as the prices of the goods bought through us and the short lead times we offer.
Thanks to the knowledge, experience and familiarity with my customers’ tastes, my company has established itself as a reliable and trustworthy partner, receiving new orders mainly through the recommendations of my existing customers’.
