RENOVATIONS

Our philosophy in life is:

„IT”S NOT A PROBLEM !” ,

Therefore, we furnish kitchens, living rooms, bathrooms and bedrooms in any style of your choice.
You can also take advantage of our professional advice and guidance in the course of building or renovating a house as well as with regards to choosing the proper construction materials.

We build, furnish, renovate and make dreams come true !

We build residential houses from scratch to turnkey ; also garages and garden houses, social facilities and workshops, beginning with groundworks, foundations, and bottom plates and finishing with the roof.

We also work build extensions, balcony constructions and terraces. We also have experience in converting attics, basements, halls and other spaces into facilities of any kind.

We guarantee modern craftsmanship, punctuality as well as a full warranty on our work because we employ qualified and experienced craftsmen thanks to whom completion of construction and conversion is accomplished in an efficient and timely manner, in accordance with the design.

In addition to construction work, we will be happy to carry out renovations, such as façades (plastering, paining, and panelling) and roofs, and installation of windows and doors.
We are a contractor for whom interior furnishing is not only work but also a challenge that we are happy to face.

For us, this is a kind of art that we feel masters of

We offer a full range of services, such as interior painting, self-levelling screeds, fitting panels and tiles,, suspended ceilings, partition walls and sanitary fixtures.

When commissioning us to complete your interior furnishing, you need not worry about anything concerning the work.
No technology or space is a barrier to us.
