MODULAR HOMES


The guiding principle of JANO BYGG AB is to make Our customers dreams come true.
That is why we would like to offer you holiday homes that have outstanding qualities.

And here are the greatest advantages
of our homes

  • 1. The homes are very ENERGY EFFICIENT due to the materials used.
    All the components from which our homes are constructed have very low thermal transmittance values
    (each wall from floor to ceiling is pre-insulated).
  • 2. The windows and doors that we install are energy efficient, with a thermal transmittance value of 1.1.
    Lower thermal transmittance values are also available at the customer’s request
    (passive houses with windows and doors installed according to customised designs).
  • 3. A unique and undeniable advantage of our houses is also their beautiful and long- lasting appearance.
    Our houses require virtually no extra financial outlay
  • 4. You SAVE on the cost of refurbishing the façades and repainting the doors and windows
    because all you have to do to refresh the appearance of your home
    is to use any commonly available detergent, e.g. Grön Såpa.

    • But that’s not all !

  • 5. We build our houses in a modular way, which means that the modules can be freely combined.
    We offer mini houses up to 25 sq m, for which you do not need any permit if you wish to assemble one on your plot, as well as houses with any size and shape.
  • 6. We have a lot of ready-made designs of houses but we also creating new designs all the time.
    We can also create individual, customised designs according to our client’s requirements.
  • 7. At the customer’s request, the house can be unfurnished or have a bathroom and kitchen that is fully equipped.

    • A large area to be furnished just as you want!

  • These are only some of the attractions; you will see there are more.
    You will be pleasantly surprised to find out about the LOW PRICE of purchase (and low cost of maintenance)
    that we offer our customers for our HOLIDAY HOMES , especially in comparison with other houses build by traditional methods.

Apart from holiday homes, we can also offer garages and carports in our system.

It takes us between 6 and 8 weeks to assemble a house and get it ready to move into, regardless of the weather !
